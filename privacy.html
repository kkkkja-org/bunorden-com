Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 23, 2026

At Bunorden, we prioritize your privacy above all else. This web application is designed to function with zero data collection. We do not track you, we do not use analytics, and we do not store any of your personal information on our servers.

Our infrastructure is built on privacy-first foundations:

  • Proton Drive: The source code and assets for this web app are stored securely on Proton Drive.
  • Cloudflare: We use Cloudflare for secure, high-performance hosting and protection. While we do not use analytics ourselves, Cloudflare Insights (static.cloudflareinsights.com) may be active as part of Cloudflare's hosting services and could be considered tracking content.

We use a single cookie/local storage entry solely to remember your timetable preferences and whether you have accepted this policy. This data remains on your device and is never sent to us.

Since we do not collect any data, there is no data to sell, share, or lose.

We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Given our zero-data collection policy, no child data is ever processed or stored.

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at contact@bunorden.com.
